Griffin Egg Omelette with Herbs

Description

"A great way to use spare healing herbs as you'll be needing them after you obtain the eggs. Don't forget to add salt."

-Nodrix the Slimy, town blacksmith

READY IN: 15 mins

SERVES: 8

Ingredients

  • 6 griffin eggs
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 2 cups parsley, chopped
  • 2 cups chives, chopped
  • 2 cups feverfew, chopped
  • 2 cups mandrake root, chopped
  • 4 cups butter
  • coriander, to garnish (optional)

Directions

  1. In a gargantuan bowl whisk eggs and then season with salt and pepper.
  2. Stir in herbs (parsley - mandrake root or your favorites).
  3. In an average sized metal shield, melt the butter over your campfire and add the egg mixture to shield.
  4. Let eggs set around edges and then lift edges and tilt shield, so uncooked eggs will go under the cooked eggs.
  5. Cook until eggs are almost set (1 -2 minutes) and then using a spatula, roll 1/3 of omelette to center.
  6. Tilt shield and slide omlet onto whatever you're eating on.
  7. Garnish with coriander, if desired.
